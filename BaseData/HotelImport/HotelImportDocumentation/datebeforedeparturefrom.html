Date from when the booking can be made, for this rule to be relevant
Either a early booking rule has a DaysBeforeDepartureFrom and DaysBeforeDepartureTo, or it has DateBeforeDepartureFrom and DateBeforeDepartureTo. They are mutually exclusive.
