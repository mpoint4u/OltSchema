Documentation: 
Arrival date for this time is the day before the departure date: -1; Arrival on the same day as the departure: 0; Arrival on the day after departure: 1; Arrival two days after departure: 2. If DossierItem/BeginDate=DossierItem/EndDate then the DayIndicator will be 0. Normally in these cases the DossierItem/EndDate and DossierItem/Duration nodes will not be sent in a response XML, and are not requires for a request XML.
